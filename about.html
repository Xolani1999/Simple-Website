About Us

We Are Ready To Make Solutions For You.

Power Transformation Balance pty ltd is a company that aims to void the gap left by the apartheid era. It is one of the fast growing company in South Africa and has already partnered with well-known companies/organization in some of our projects. We aim to archieve the following each and every year:

Migrating small businesses to the world of technology using our IT solutions.

Offer free skill trainings to over 500 individuals every year.

Easy access of cleaning services in all rural areas.

Provide sanitary pads, food parcels, clothes and other donations to over 10 000 individuals each year.
